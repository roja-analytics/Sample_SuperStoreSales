# 📊 Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end sales performance analysis using the Sample Superstore dataset. The analysis was conducted using SQL Server, Python, and Power BI to uncover business insights related to regional performance, product profitability, customer segments, and seasonal sales trends. The interactive dashboard helps stakeholders make data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The project aims to answer the following business questions:

- Which regions are underperforming?
- Is there a seasonal pattern in sales?
- Which product category should be prioritized?
- Which products generate the highest sales and profit?
- How do customer segments contribute to overall sales?

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- The West region generated the highest overall sales and profit.
- Technology was the most profitable product category.
- Higher discounts negatively impacted profitability.
- Consumer customers contributed the highest sales.
- Sales showed seasonal peaks during year-end months.
- Some products generated high sales but low profit, highlighting opportunities for pricing optimization.
